# Agentic Design Patterns (智能体设计模式)

> Survey date: 2026-03-05.

---

**Core idea:** Four foundational patterns for building AI agent workflows:
1. **Reflection** — AI critiques its own output and iterates to improve
2. **Tool Use** — AI calls APIs and interacts with external systems
3. **Planning** — Complex tasks decomposed into sequential steps
4. **Multi-Agent Collaboration** — Multiple specialized agents work together

**Key proponent:** Andrew Ng (DeepLearning.AI, March 2024 onwards; full course launched 2025).

**Key insight from Ng:** "The single biggest predictor of success is the ability to drive a disciplined process for evals and error analysis."

**Why it matters:** 93% of IT leaders plan to introduce autonomous agents within 2 years. Deloitte predicts 25% of GenAI companies launched agentic pilots by 2025, growing to 50% by 2027. Frameworks: LangChain, AutoGen, CrewAI, OpenAI Swarm.
