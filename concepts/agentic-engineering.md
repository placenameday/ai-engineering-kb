# Vibe Coding → Agentic Engineering (氛围编程 → 智能体工程)

> Survey date: 2026-03-05.

---

**Core idea (Vibe Coding):** "Fully give in to the vibes, embrace exponentials, and forget that the code even exists." Accept all AI-generated code without reviewing diffs, paste errors back to AI for resolution, let codebase grow beyond comprehension.

**Core idea (Agentic Engineering):** The matured successor (2026). Not writing code directly 99% of the time; instead orchestrating AI agents with human oversight and quality standards intact. "Engineering" emphasizes art, science, and expertise.

**Key proponent:** Andrej Karpathy (coined "vibe coding" Feb 2025; proposed "agentic engineering" early 2026).

**Cultural impact:** Named Collins Dictionary Word of the Year 2025. Merriam-Webster listed it March 2025.

**Why it matters:** Represents the spectrum from amateur AI-assisted development to professional AI-orchestrated engineering. CodeRabbit analysis of 470 PRs found AI co-authored code had 1.7x more "major" issues — highlighting why the shift from "vibe" to "engineering" discipline matters.
