# Agentic Engineering Patterns (智能体工程模式 — Willison)

> Survey date: 2026-03-05.

---

**Core idea:** Documented coding practices and patterns for getting the best results from coding agents (Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, etc.). Positions AI-assisted development as expertise amplification, not replacement.

**Key proponent:** Simon Willison (Django co-creator, Datasette founder). Publishing 1-2 chapters/week as of Feb 2026.

**Key concepts from Willison:**
- **Context offloading** — moving state out of unpredictable prompts into durable storage
- **Prompt injection as "SQL injection of our day"** — treating AI security with the same seriousness
- **YOLO mode** — acknowledges productivity boost but warns of security risks
- **Agentic Engineering** as "the professional's approach" — distinct from vibe coding

**Why it matters:** Willison is widely regarded as the most reliable "ground truth" voice on what's actually happening in generative AI. His patterns bridge theory and daily practice.
